The problem

AI courses have one of two problems. Claude Mastery fixes both.

You either get generic "prompt engineering" content that teaches nothing specific, or developer-first deep dives that assume you already ship production software. Neither is written for the people who stand to benefit most.

Option A

Generic AI courses

"Here are 50 ChatGPT prompts" lists. Watchable. Forgettable. Nothing you learn survives contact with your actual job on Monday morning.

Option B

Developer-first deep dives

API keys, agents, function-calling, RAG. Incredible depth — wrong audience. If you don't already live in a terminal, you bounce in the first hour.

The third way

Personalised, graded, non-technical.

Real Claude work, tailored to your industry. Every deliverable is AI-graded against a 4-criteria rubric. Built to be finished, not just bought.

Why this works

Four design choices that make the difference.

Most courses fail the same way: you finish week one feeling inspired, then drift. Every decision here is aimed at keeping you in — and making sure the work you do is the work you actually need at your desk.

Frequently asked

The honest answers, before you buy.

When does my access actually start?
Access opens on 1 June 2026. You won't be able to log in before then — I'm still building. You'll get your login email on launch day to the address you used at Stripe checkout.
Why no testimonials?
Because there are no students yet. You'd be joining the founding cohort — the first people through the course — at a price that won't exist again. I won't fabricate quotes.
Can I get a refund?
Yes — full refund, no questions, anytime before 1 June 2026. Once access starts and you can see the lessons, the course is a digital product you've consumed — no refunds from that point. This doesn't affect your rights under Australian Consumer Law.
What if you miss the 1 June launch?
I'll email you first — before anyone else — with the new date and the reason. If the new date doesn't work for you, you can take the pre-launch refund.
Is there a payment plan?
Yes — via Afterpay, Klarna or Zip at Stripe checkout, split into 4 × $74.25 interest-free payments. Or pay in full in one go. Whatever works for you.
Do I need a Claude account?
Yes — free or paid both work. Module 01 includes a 10-minute walkthrough to set this up before the first graded lesson. If you already use Claude, you're set.
Do I need any AI or coding experience to start?
No. Core is built specifically for people who are curious about AI but feel every existing course is either too light or too technical. Module 01 assumes zero. By the end of Module 02 you'll be writing better prompts than most people who've used Claude for a year.
What if I can't pass a deliverable?
You can resubmit as many times as you want, with a 24-hour cooldown between attempts. The grading engine gives written feedback explaining exactly what would move you from 68 to 82. Nobody fails out of Core — some people just take longer to finish.
How is Claude Mastery different from free content on YouTube?
Free content shows you what's possible. Claude Mastery makes you actually do it. Every lesson has a deliverable. Every deliverable is graded. You can't skim your way through — and because it's personalised to your industry, you leave with real assets for your business, not demo prompts.
What does "completing Core" actually mean?
Finishing all 34 lessons, passing every graded deliverable, and submitting the Core Capstone. Most people finish in 20–30 hours over 4–8 weeks at their own pace. Once complete, you get a Completion Statement and priority invitation to the next tier.
What does "personalised to my industry" mean in practice?
Six verticals on launch day: agency/marketing, trades and services, health and wellness, finance and accounting, retail and e-commerce, property and real estate. You pick one at onboarding. Every example, prompt, and deliverable is re-rendered for your industry — not retrofitted, actually written for your world.
